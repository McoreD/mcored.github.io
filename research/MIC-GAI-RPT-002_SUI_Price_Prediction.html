The Bear Case & Key Risks

Token Unlock Headwinds

SUI faces significant, scheduled token unlocks throughout 2025, creating a persistent source of potential sell pressure. While these unlocks provide necessary liquidity for institutional buyers, they require continuous strong demand to be absorbed without suppressing the price.

Premium Valuation & Execution Risk

SUI's high valuation relative to its current fee generation demands near-flawless execution of its ambitious roadmap. The project also operates in a fiercely competitive market and is not immune to potential security vulnerabilities.

High P/F Multiple: SUI's Price-to-Fees multiple of 636x is significantly higher than Solana's 67x, pricing in massive future growth.

Security Exploits: As a complex system, SUI remains at risk of bugs or exploits that could damage investor confidence.
